About Us

Kashmir Harvard is a prestigious and innovative institution dedicated to providing a world-class education and fostering an intellectually stimulating environment for our students.

Established in the year 1956, we have a rich history of academic excellence and a strong commitment to shaping future leaders and thinkers.

At Kashmir Harvard, we are committed to providing a prestigious and innovative institution that offers a world-class education and fosters an intellectually stimulating environment for all our students. Established in the year 1956, we take pride in our rich history of academic excellence and our strong dedication to shaping future leaders and thinkers.

Our school is more than just a place of learning; it is a vibrant community that encourages personal growth, intellectual curiosity, and social responsibility. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who not only excel in academics but also in extracurricular activities, sports, and creative endeavors.

Our campus is a haven for learning and exploration, with state-of-the-art facilities that enhance the overall educational experience. Our committed and experienced faculty members are passionate about teaching and guiding students to reach their full potential.

At Kashmir Harvard, we understand the importance of a holistic education. Along with a robust academic curriculum, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, clubs, and sports programs that allow students to discover and develop their unique talents and interests.

As a school that values diversity and inclusivity, we celebrate the uniqueness of each individual and create an environment that promotes mutual respect, understanding, and open-mindedness.

We encourage active parent involvement in their child's educational journey, believing that a strong partnership between parents, teachers, and students contributes to the overall success of our students.

Whether you are a prospective student, parent, or visitor, we invite you to explore our website to learn more about Kashmir Harvard School and the exceptional opportunities we offer. We look forward to welcoming you to our esteemed institution and shaping a bright future together.
