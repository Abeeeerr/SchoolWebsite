Our Extracurricular Activities

At Kashmir Harvard, we believe in providing a holistic education that goes beyond textbooks and classrooms. Our Extracurricular Activities Program offers a wide range of exciting and enriching experiences that allow students to explore their interests, develop new skills, and foster a sense of adventure.

Through our Extracurricular Activities Program, we aim to inspire a sense of adventure and curiosity in our students, empowering them to explore their passions and interests beyond the classroom. Join us on this exciting journey of exploration and discovery! Whether you have a love for nature, a passion for photography, or a desire to challenge yourself in the pool, our extracurricular activities have something for everyone. Come, be a part of our vibrant and engaging extracurricular community and unlock your full potential at Kashmir Harvard!

Clubs

Welcome to the exciting world of clubs at our school! We believe in nurturing our students' diverse interests and talents, and our extensive range of extracurricular activities plays a crucial role in shaping well-rounded individuals. Our school offers a vibrant selection of clubs, each designed to inspire creativity, foster teamwork, and ignite passion.

These clubs are more than just extracurricular activities; they are spaces where friendships are forged, skills are honed, and passions are kindled. We encourage every student to explore and join the clubs that resonate with their interests, as we believe that extracurricular engagement enriches the educational experience and nurtures well-rounded individuals.
